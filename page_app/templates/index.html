MY PERSONAL STORY...

After graduating high school, I became an Emergecny Medical Technician, and completed a fire academy. I have since spent the past 14 years as a first responder. When I wasn't working, I used to spend my time weightlifting, rock climbing, fishing, and and backpacking, occassionally tackling the summit of a 14er.


More recently, however, I became a father of the best, but most difficult little boy I can imagine. Now my free time consists of fuelling my coffee addiction to overcome my exhaustion, and making sure I give my son, Jax, the best life possible. Our days generally involve a park visit, a bike ride, playtime with problem solving toys, and Jax stealing my food, because his is apparently never good enough. I am transitioning out of my role as a first responder, so I can provide a better life for my family.
